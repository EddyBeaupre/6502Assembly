EOR, 6502 EOR - Exclusive OR

Information about the 6502 EOR Instruction
EOR --- EOR stands for Exclusive OR (with Accumulator)
EOR is one of the Logical operations in 6502 Instruction-set. EOR operations perform a bitwise logical exclusive-OR between the content of Accumulator and content specified in specific location. There are seven opcodes of EOR representing seven different addressing mode.
