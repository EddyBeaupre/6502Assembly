TAY - 6502 TAY, Transfer Accumulator to Y

Information about the 6502 TAY Instruction

TAY    --- TAY stands for  Transfer Accumulator to Y
TAY (Transfer Accumulator to Y) is one of the Register transfer operations in 6502 instruction-set. The function of TAY is to transfer the current contents of Accumulator into the Index Register Y.
