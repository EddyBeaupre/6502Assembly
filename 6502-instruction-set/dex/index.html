DEX - 6502 DEX, Decrement x

Information about the 6502 DEX Instruction

DEX --- DEX stands for Decrement
DEX is one of the instruction of the Arithmetic Operations of 6502 Instruction set. The main functionality of DEX is to subtract Register X by one.
