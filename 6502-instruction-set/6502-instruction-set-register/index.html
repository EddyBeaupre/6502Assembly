6502 Register Transfer Operations

Register Transfer Operations of 6502 instruction set

Registers are the fastest memory in a microprocessor. Register Transfer Operations are usually used for the preparation of other instructions. The Register Transfer Operations are fast (usually requires only two cycles) and the addressing mode simple.  There are six Register transfer operations in 6502 instruction-set:
  1. TAX    ---    Transfer Accumulator to X
  2. TAY    ---    Transfer Accumulator to Y
  3. TXA    ---    Transfer X to Accumulator  
  4. TYA    ---    Transfer Y to Accumulator
  5. TSX    ---    Transfer Stack pointer to X
  6. TXS    ---    Transfer X to Stack pointer
